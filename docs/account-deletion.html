Delete your ImHim account & data

Last updated: 20 June 2026

ImHim (Android package name com.mirrorly.mirrorly, previously published as “Mirrorly”) is operated by M2MB. This page explains how to delete your ImHim account and the data ImHim has processed about you.

About ImHim accounts

ImHim does not require you to create a username or password to use the app. Your purchases, saved scans, and on-device history are tied to your Google Play (or Apple App Store) account for billing and to your individual installation on your device. There is no separate ImHim login.

Because there is no remote account, all of your ImHim data lives in three places only:

  • On your device — your scan JPEGs, scores, preferences, streak progress, rizz memory, and any saved Mirror renders are stored locally in the ImHim app container.
  • On your Google Play / Apple App Store account — your subscription status, billing history, and renewal preferences.
  • Transiently on M2MB’s backend — when you tap a feature that fires an AI request (a scan, a Mirror render, a rizz reply, a roleplay turn), photo bytes or audio bytes pass through M2MB’s backend in-memory to the AI provider (OpenAI / Replicate) and back. M2MB does not persist those bytes; only timestamps and HTTP status codes are retained for 30 days for diagnostics, then auto-expired.

How to delete your ImHim account and data — in-app (recommended)

  1. Open ImHim on your device.
  2. Tap the cog icon in the top-right of the Looks tab to open Settings.
  3. Scroll to the bottom of the Settings list.
  4. Tap Delete my account.
  5. Confirm the destructive dialog.

This action wipes:

  • Every saved scan JPEG (on-device).
  • Every persisted scan record, score, and protocol streak (on-device).
  • The on-device rizz memory layer.
  • Every cached AI-render URL.
  • Every locally-stored consent flag and setting.

The deletion is immediate and irreversible. There is nothing held on M2MB’s backend that needs separate deletion because nothing was ever persisted there.

Cancelling your subscription

Deleting your ImHim account in-app does not cancel your active Google Play (or Apple App Store) subscription. Billing is managed by Google / Apple, not by M2MB. To cancel:

  • Android: Google Play → Profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → ImHim → Cancel subscription.
  • iOS: Settings app → Apple ID (your name) → Subscriptions → ImHim → Cancel subscription.

You may cancel either before or after deleting your in-app data. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period; partial-period refunds are handled by Google or Apple at their discretion.

If you have already uninstalled ImHim

Uninstalling the app removes the on-device data automatically. There is no remaining M2MB-side account to delete because M2MB never held one. If you still want a written confirmation that nothing remains, email the address below.

We will respond within 30 days confirming what has been deleted (in practice, nothing M2MB-side because we do not persist your data; we will confirm that fact in writing).
