## Description (max 4000 chars)

BECOME THE MAN WHO OWNS THE ROOM

Most guys freeze. They overthink the text, dodge the approach, and replay the
moment they missed for a week. ImHim is the training ground that fixes that —
with real reps, not theory.

Practice real conversations. Run daily missions. Watch yourself turn into the
guy who walks in calm, confident, and unbothered.

PRACTICE THAT FEELS REAL
Text or call ten distinct AI characters — each with her own personality, mood,
and standards. The warm one. The one who tests you. The ice-cold one you have
to earn. They remember you and pick up where you left off, so every
conversation builds on the last. Low stakes, real practice, zero fear of
embarrassment.

DAILY MISSIONS THAT PUSH YOU
Three practice missions and two real-world missions every day — from commenting
on one post, to holding eye contact, to walking up and starting a real
conversation. It starts accessible and gets bolder fast. Your streak stays
alive as long as you show up; only the real reps move your score.

A 60-DAY JOURNEY, NOT A CHECKLIST
Follow the Ascension Map from Day 1 to Day 60 — Observer to Him. Every mission
moves you one step up the path. Unlock new characters, harder challenges, and
your final "Become Him" card as you climb. You always see exactly where you
stand and who you're becoming.

GET SCORED. GET BETTER.
Every voice conversation is scored across five dimensions — Confidence,
Presence, Game, Humour, and Listening — with an honest breakdown of what landed
and what flopped. Watch the numbers climb over 60 days.

YOUR POCKET COACH
Stuck on a text? Get the exact line. Facing an approach? Get a game plan. Drop
a screenshot of a conversation and get three replies that actually sound like
you.

WHY IT WORKS
Confidence isn't something you read your way into — it's reps you bank. ImHim
gives you a safe place to practice, a reason to show up every single day, and a
clear path from where you are now to the man who never misses the moment.

Put in the reps. Become Him.
