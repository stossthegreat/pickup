Delete Your Data

Last updated 18 July 2026

ImHim has no account and no login, so there is no account to delete — but you are always in full control of your data and can erase it completely at any time.

Delete everything from the app (instant)

Almost everything ImHim knows about you — your streak, scores, XP, progress, and every character conversation — is stored on your device. To wipe all of it:

  1. Open ImHim.
  2. Go to Settings (the gear icon).
  3. Tap Delete all data and confirm.

This permanently erases your on-device data. Deleting the app from your device also removes all of it.

Request deletion by email

ImHim does not store your conversations on our servers, and our analytics is anonymous. If you would still like us to delete or confirm the removal of any data associated with your device, email us and we'll action it within 30 days:

Email info@m2mb.co.uk
Subject: Delete my data
Please send from the request and, if you have it, include the approximate date you first used the app so we can locate any anonymous records.

Cancelling your subscription

Deleting your data does not cancel an active subscription, and cancelling a subscription does not delete your data. To cancel ImHim Pro, open your device App Store → account → Subscriptions and turn off ImHim. Subscriptions are managed by Apple.

What gets deleted

  • Deleted from your device: streak, ascension progress, scores, XP, unlocked characters, and all conversation memory.
  • Anonymous analytics: removed on request; it contains no name, email, or contact details.
  • Subscription record: held by Apple; manage it in your App Store settings.
