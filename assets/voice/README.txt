AURALAY — Voice Coach Clips
============================

The viral seduction test, charisma test, and seduction lesson all play
AI-directed voice cues at phase transitions + on signal-fail events.
Drop your recorded MP3s into this directory using the EXACT filenames
below.

Each clip should be:
  - MP3, 128 kbps mono, ~22 kHz
  - 1.0–2.5 seconds long
  - Recorded in a low room with minimal reverb
  - Voice tone: charisma coach — direct, confident, slightly low

Every test FUNCTIONS without the clips (silent fallback, captions still
show on screen). Adding clips just upgrades the experience.
