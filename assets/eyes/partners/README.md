# Eye-contact training partners — dedicated renders

Three partners rotated across the 12 gaze lessons. These are NOT the same
brief as the Game arena women — different framing and intent.

| File | Lessons | Brief |
|---|---|---|
| `neutral.jpg` | 1 – 4 (The Lock, The Extend, The Shift, The Smile Hold) | Approachable brunette, soft lighting, steady gaze. |
| `sharp.jpg`   | 5 – 8 | Cool blonde, harder gaze, low-key dramatic light. |
| `playful.jpg` | 9 – 12 | Warm raven or redhead, slight smile in the eyes. |

Format: JPEG, 3:4 portrait, 1500 × 2000 px, sRGB, ~85% quality, ≤ 400 KB.

Art direction — different from Game arena, this is gaze training:
- TIGHT crop: face + neck only, NO bar / club / environment
- Eyes positioned at exactly 1/3 from top of frame
- Dead-on camera, very slightly above eye level (forces user to look up)
- Expression MINIMAL: neutral mouth, present eyes — not flirty, not smouldering
- Background near-black, soft warm-red rim on one cheek
- Optional v2: 4-second silent video loop with one natural blink at second 3
